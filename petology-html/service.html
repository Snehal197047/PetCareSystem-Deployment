Our Services

Consultation

Our hospital is equipped to provide diagnostic and therapeutic services to care for your pets complete health care needs along with perfect Consultation.

Surgeries(soft & hard tissues)

Our surgical suite provides for the performance of a wide variety of surgical procedures.

Treatment

We ensure proper safety and make sure that your pet gets the best possible treatment.

Xrays

Our modern X-ray equipment provides high quality radiographs to quickly find the disorders.

Vaccination & Deworming

We utilize the safest available anesthetics to provide an extra safety, especially for our older or high-risk patients.

Microchipping

A microchip is a radio-frequency identification transponder that carries a unique identification number, and is roughly the size of a grain of rice.

Laboratory Diagnostics

Our laboratory facility provides serum chemistry, hematology, serology, urinalysis and parasite testing.

Emergency OPD

We provide medical services to pets suffering from sudden disorders or sudden changes in existing medical conditions outside of opening hours.

Pet Food & Accessories

Customers can purchase pet food, medicines, all kinds of Accessories which are required for the pets.

House & Farm Visits

We provide emergency as well as house and farms visits to treat your pets like horses, cows, goats, etc with proper precautions.
